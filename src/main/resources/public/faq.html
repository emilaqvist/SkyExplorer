Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I search for flights?

Simply enter your departure city, destination, and travel dates, then click the "Search Flights" button. We’ll show you all available flights based on your input!

Can I book flights directly through SkyExplorer?

Not yet! Right now, we provide you with the best flight options, but booking needs to be done through our partner airlines.

What happens if no flights are found?

If no flights match your search, you’ll see a message saying "No flights found." Try adjusting your dates or choosing a nearby airport.

How do I check the weather at my destination?

After searching for a flight, the weather for your destination will automatically appear on the results page.

How accurate is the weather forecast?

We use data from a trusted weather API, which updates regularly. However, actual weather conditions may change unexpectedly.

Can I check the weather for a different city?

Currently, the weather feature only shows forecasts for the destination you searched for.

How do I see attractions at my destination?

After your flight search, click on the button "Utforska ..." . There, you'll see top attractions and sightseeing suggestions for that city.

Where does SkyExplorer get attraction recommendations from?

Our recommendations come from various travel and tourism sources (OpenTripMap API and Wikipedia), ensuring you see the best-rated places.

Can I book tours and activities directly?

At this time, we only provide recommendations. However, we are working on adding direct booking options in the future.
